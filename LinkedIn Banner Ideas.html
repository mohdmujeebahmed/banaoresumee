What makes a LinkedIn banner effective

What makes a LinkedIn banner effective

A strong banner reinforces your headline and profile, rather than sitting empty or generic.

01

Correct dimensions, no stretching

1584 x 396 pixels is the standard size — anything off-ratio appears stretched or cropped oddly.

02

Reinforces your role or field

A subtle visual cue about your industry or specialty adds context beyond just your headline text.

03

Not competing with your profile photo

Keep the area behind your profile picture (bottom-left) relatively clear so your photo stands out clearly.

Examples

LinkedIn banner ideas by career stage

Different approaches depending on your field and career stage.

Students/Freshers
A simple, clean banner with your university name/logo area and field of study, in your school's or a neutral professional color scheme.
Job Seekers
A minimalist banner stating your target role or specialty (e.g. "Aspiring Data Analyst") in clean, readable typography.
Experienced Professionals
A banner reflecting your industry — subtle product screenshots, a relevant skyline, or your company/personal brand colors.
Mistakes

Common LinkedIn banner mistakes

✕

Leaving the default blue banner

An empty default banner is a missed opportunity to reinforce your professional brand.

✕

Using a stretched or low-resolution image

Off-ratio images appear blurry or distorted — always use the correct 1584x396 dimensions.

✕

Overcrowding with too much text

A banner with dense text is hard to read at a glance — keep any text short and large.

Pro tips

Pro tips for your LinkedIn banner

Use free tools with LinkedIn-sized templates

Canva and similar tools offer pre-sized LinkedIn banner templates to simplify design.

Keep your profile photo area clear

Avoid placing important banner content directly behind where your circular profile photo sits.

Match your banner to your headline

A banner that visually reinforces your stated role or field feels more cohesive.

FAQ

LinkedIn banner — FAQ

What is the correct LinkedIn banner size?

1584 x 396 pixels is the standard, recommended size for a LinkedIn profile banner image.

Do I need a custom banner, or is the default fine?

A custom banner is recommended — it's a simple way to reinforce your professional brand instead of leaving default blank space.

Can I use a photo I already have as my banner?

Yes, as long as it can be cropped to the correct 1584x396 ratio without becoming stretched or pixelated.

Should freshers have a LinkedIn banner too?

Yes — even a simple, clean banner reflecting your field of study adds polish to a fresher's profile.
