Mistakes

Common cover letter template mistakes

✕

Using the same template with no customization

Always personalize the company, role, and one specific reason for applying.

✕

Repeating your resume word for word

A cover letter should add context and story, not restate your bullet points.

✕

Making it longer than one page

Keep it to 3-4 tight paragraphs — hiring managers rarely read past that.

Pro tips

Pro tips for using a cover letter template

Personalize the opening line every time

Naming the exact role and company signals genuine, not mass-sent, interest.

Lead with your strongest proof point

Put your best, most relevant achievement early, not buried in the middle.

Match tone to the company

A startup may welcome a more casual tone; a bank or law firm expects more formal language.
