Questions Designers Actually Ask

Do UI/UX designers need an ATS-friendly resume or just a portfolio? +

Both. Most companies still run resumes through an Applicant Tracking System before a human designer or recruiter ever opens your portfolio link. If your resume isn't parsed correctly, your beautiful Figma case studies never get seen.

Should my UX resume be visually designed or plain text? +

Keep your resume clean and ATS-safe — save the visual storytelling for your portfolio. Recruiters and ATS software often reject heavily graphic resumes because the parser cannot read text inside images, icons, or complex columns.

Is BanaoResume free for designers? +

Yes. BanaoResume is a free ATS resume builder for students and freshers, including UI/UX design freshers and career-switchers, with no signup wall for the core builder.

What should I include instead of a work experience section if I have none? +

Use a Projects or Case Studies section instead. List 2-4 projects with the problem, your role, tools used, and the measurable outcome (e.g. reduced task completion time, improved usability score, increased conversion in a redesign).
