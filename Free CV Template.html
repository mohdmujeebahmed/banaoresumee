Mistakes

Common CV template mistakes

✕

Using a template with heavy graphics

Even for longer CVs, visual clutter reduces both readability and ATS compatibility.

✕

Inconsistent formatting across pages

Font size, spacing, and heading style should stay identical throughout a multi-page CV.

✕

Leaving placeholder text in the final document

Always double-check every section for leftover sample or placeholder content before submitting.

Pro tips

Pro tips for using a CV template

Adjust section order to your strengths

Move your strongest section (e.g. publications for academia) higher if it's especially strong.

Keep formatting locked once chosen

Avoid switching fonts or spacing mid-document as you fill in content.

Review length against norms in your field

Academic and research CVs can run longer; industry CVs should stay more concise.
