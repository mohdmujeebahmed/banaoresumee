Common mistakes

What gets Infosys resumes filtered out

  • ✕Tables, columns, or graphics. Many ATS parsers misread multi-column layouts and drop entire sections of text.
  • ✕Vague tech stack. "Worked with various technologies" gives the system nothing to match against the posting.
  • ✕Certifications left off. Cloud or Infosys-specific certifications omitted entirely, even when the applicant holds them.
  • ✕Generic job titles. "IT professional" instead of the posting's actual title reduces keyword match score.
  • ✕No measurable outcome. Bullets without a metric or scope read as unverifiable and get skipped in a fast recruiter scan.
Interview tips

Once your resume gets through, prep for this

01

Expect technical and HR rounds

Infosys interviews typically run through a technical round on your stack, followed by an HR round on fit and communication.

02

Know your project details cold

Be ready to explain the architecture, your specific role, and challenges on any project listed on your resume in detail.

03

Practice fundamentals, not just frameworks

Data structures, OOP concepts, and DBMS basics come up often, even for experienced roles — don't skip revisiting them.

04

Ask about client and location flexibility

Project staffing can involve relocation or client-site work — clarify expectations early rather than after an offer.
