About BanaoResume

Free, simple, and built for students across India 🇮🇳

What is BanaoResume?

BanaoResume is a free online resume builder designed specifically for students, freshers, and early-career professionals in India. We help you create clean, ATS-friendly resumes in minutes — no sign-up required, no hidden fees, ever.

Why we built this

Most resume builders are either too expensive, too complicated, or not suited for Indian job markets. We wanted to change that. BanaoResume is built with one goal: help every student land their first job without worrying about the cost of a resume tool.

What you get — 100% Free

  • 6 professionally designed templates
  • ATS-optimized formatting for Indian recruiters
  • Instant PDF download — colored or black & white
  • No account needed, no data stored on servers
  • Works on mobile, tablet, and desktop

Your data is yours

Everything you type stays in your browser. We don't store your resume data on any server. When you close the tab, your data is gone — completely private.

Support this project ☕

BanaoResume is free and will always be free. If it helped you land a job or internship, consider supporting us via PhonePe — it helps keep the servers running and motivates us to add more templates and features.
