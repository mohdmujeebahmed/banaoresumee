Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers before you get started

Is my data safe? Does BanaoResume store my information?+
Your privacy is our top priority. All resume data is processed entirely in your browser and is never sent to our servers. When you close the tab, your data is gone. We only collect anonymous analytics via Google Analytics to improve the site.
Is downloading my resume free for all templates?+
Yes! All 3 templates — Modern Orange, ATS Minimal, and IIT-to-Google Format — are completely free to download as PDF. No sign-up, no hidden fees, ever.
What is ATS and why does it matter?+
ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System — software used by most companies to automatically screen resumes before a human ever sees them. BanaoResume templates are designed with clean formatting, standard section headers, and readable fonts to pass ATS scans.
I'm a fresher with no work experience. Can I still make a good resume?+
Absolutely! Choose the "Fresher" mode and BanaoResume will prioritise your Projects, Skills, and Education sections. Strong projects, internships, or open-source contributions can make your resume stand out.
Does BanaoResume work on mobile?+
Yes, BanaoResume is fully responsive and works on Android and iOS. However, for downloading as PDF, we recommend using a desktop or laptop browser (Chrome or Edge) for the cleanest print output.
How do the paid services work?+
Click "Chat on WhatsApp" on any service, send us your resume, and we'll get back to you within 24 hours with payment details and a timeline. Simple — no subscriptions, no portals, just a real human helping you.

Free, simple, and built for students across India 🇮🇳

What is BanaoResume?

BanaoResume is a free online resume builder designed specifically for students, freshers, and early-career professionals in India. We help you create clean, ATS-friendly resumes in minutes — no sign-up required, no hidden fees, ever.

Why we built this

Most resume builders are either too expensive, too complicated, or not suited for Indian job markets. BanaoResume is built with one goal: help every student land their first job.

What you get — 100% Free

  • 3 professionally designed templates including the viral IIT-to-Google format
  • ATS-optimized formatting for Indian recruiters
  • Instant PDF download — colored or black & white
  • No account needed, no data stored on servers
