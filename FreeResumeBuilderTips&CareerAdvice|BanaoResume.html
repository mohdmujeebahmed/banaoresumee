⚡ Quick Resume Tips That Actually Work

Evidence-backed tips from recruiters at Google, Microsoft, Amazon & top Indian companies

Tip 01 — Contact Information

Add a LinkedIn URL & GitHub (if applicable)

Resumes with a complete LinkedIn profile link get 40% more recruiter responses. Make sure your LinkedIn headline matches your resume title exactly — consistency builds credibility.

Tip 02 — Resume Summary

Write a 3-line professional summary, not an objective

Objective statements are outdated. A modern resume summary shows who you are, what you've done, and what value you bring — in under 50 words. Lead with your biggest achievement.

Tip 03 — Work Experience

Use the X-Y-Z formula for every bullet point

"Accomplished [X] as measured by [Y] by doing [Z]." Example: "Increased sales by 32% in Q3 by launching a targeted email campaign for high-value customers." Quantify everything you can.

Tip 04 — ATS Optimization

Mirror exact keywords from the job description

Copy and paste the job description into a word-frequency tool. The top nouns and verbs are your ATS keywords. Include them naturally in your summary, skills section, and experience bullets.

Tip 05 — Resume Length

Keep it to 1 page (under 10 years experience)

Unless you're a senior leader or academic, a one-page resume is the gold standard. Use tight margins (0.5"), 10-11pt font, and cut any experience older than 10 years ruthlessly.

Tip 06 — File Format

Always submit a .PDF — unless told otherwise

PDFs preserve your formatting across all devices and operating systems. Word documents (.docx) can shift fonts and margins. Name your file: FirstName-LastName-Resume.pdf for professionalism.

✅ Resume Writing Checklist — Before You Submit

Run through this quick checklist every time you send out a job application. Recruiters notice these details.

📧

Professional email address

firstname.lastname@gmail.com — not nicknames or numbers from 2008.

📍

City & state (not full address)

Bengaluru, Karnataka is enough. Full addresses look dated and waste space.

📁

Correct file name

Save as "FirstName-LastName-Resume.pdf" — never "Resume Final v3 NEW.docx".

🔢

Quantify achievements

Every bullet should answer "so what?" with a number, percentage, or dollar amount.

🔤

Consistent formatting

Same font family, date format, and bullet style throughout. Inconsistency signals carelessness.

🔍

Spell check & grammar

Read it backwards to catch typos. Ask a friend to review. A single typo can disqualify you.

🤖

ATS keywords included

Match at least 70% of the key terms from the job description in your resume.

📏

Right length

1 page for <10 years experience. 2 pages max for senior roles. Never 3+ pages.

🎨

Clean, readable design

No fancy graphics, tables, or text boxes that ATS systems can't read. Simple is powerful.

Frequently Asked Questions

Resume FAQ

How long should a resume be in 2025?
For most job seekers with under 10 years of experience, one page is ideal. Senior professionals (10+ years) may use two pages if every line adds real value. Academic CVs can be longer. The rule: include only what helps you get the interview.
What is an ATS-friendly resume?
An ATS (Applicant Tracking System) friendly resume uses simple formatting, standard section headings, and job-specific keywords. Avoid tables, text boxes, headers/footers, and graphics. Use .PDF or .DOCX as specified by the employer. BanaoResume templates are all ATS-tested.
Should I include a photo on my resume?
In India, a professional photo is commonly accepted and often expected. In the US, UK, Canada, and Australia, photos are typically omitted to prevent unconscious bias during hiring. Follow the norms of the country you're applying to.
How do I write a resume with no experience?
Focus on transferable skills, academic projects, internships, certifications, and volunteer work. Use a functional or skills-based resume format. Lead with a strong summary that highlights your potential. A fresher resume should emphasize relevant coursework and achievements.
What's the best resume format for freshers in India?
A reverse-chronological format with a strong education section at the top works best. Include projects, internships, skills, and certifications. Keep it to one page. Use clean, ATS-compatible templates available free on BanaoResume.
Is it necessary to write a cover letter?
While not always mandatory, a cover letter significantly increases your chances when it's thoughtfully written. It lets you explain your motivation, address gaps, and show personality. For competitive roles, always include one. For mass applications, a strong cover letter template works well.
What font should I use on my resume?
Stick to professional, ATS-readable fonts like Calibri (11pt), Arial (11pt), Garamond (11pt), or Georgia (11pt). Avoid decorative or script fonts. Use 10.5–12pt for body text and 14–16pt for your name at the top. Consistent font usage signals professionalism.
How do I tailor my resume for each job?
Read the job description carefully and mirror the exact language used for required skills and responsibilities. Adjust your professional summary to address the specific role. Move the most relevant experience to the top. This takes 10–15 minutes but can double your response rate.
