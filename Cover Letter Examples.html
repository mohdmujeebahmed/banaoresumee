Examples

Three real cover letter examples

Fresher, experienced, and career-switcher versions — see the difference in structure.

Fresher ExampleBR

Dear Hiring Manager,

I'm a final-year BCA student applying for the Software Developer Trainee role at [Company]. My final-year project, a real-time chat app built with the MERN stack, gave me hands-on experience with the technologies your job posting mentions.

I'd welcome the opportunity to bring that foundation to your engineering team.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

Experienced ExampleBR

Dear [Hiring Manager],

I'm applying for the Senior Marketing Manager role at [Company]. Over the past 4 years, I've led campaigns that grew organic traffic 65% year-over-year and managed a $200K annual ad budget.

I'm drawn to [Company]'s recent expansion into [specific area] and would welcome the chance to bring that experience to your team.

Best regards,
[Your Name]

Career Switcher ExampleBR

Dear [Hiring Manager],

After 3 years in retail management, I'm transitioning into data analytics, backed by a completed certification in SQL and Python and a self-built dashboard analyzing 50K+ sales records.

My background gives me strong business context that I believe would add real value to your analytics team.

Sincerely,
[Your Name]

Mistakes

Common mistakes when copying examples

✕

Copying an example word-for-word

Use examples for structure and tone only — always personalize the actual content.

✕

Using an experienced example when you're a fresher

Match the example's career stage to your own for a realistic, credible tone.

✕

Ignoring industry tone differences

A creative agency example may read too casual for a finance or legal application, and vice versa.

Pro tips

Pro tips for using cover letter examples

Borrow structure, not sentences

Use an example's paragraph flow and length as a guide, not literal wording.

Pick the example closest to your situation

A fresher, experienced, or switcher example will guide tone more accurately than a generic one.

Always personalize company-specific details

Swap in a real, specific reason for interest in the company you're applying to.

FAQ

Cover letter examples — FAQ

Can I copy a cover letter example directly?

No — use examples for structure and tone, but always personalize the actual content and details for your situation.

What makes a cover letter example "good"?

Specificity — a real achievement, a genuine reason for interest, and a clear, confident close, all within a concise length.

Are cover letter examples different by industry?

Yes — tone and formality often shift between creative, corporate, technical, and academic fields.
